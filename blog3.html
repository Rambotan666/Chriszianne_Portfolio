Balancing Studies and Passion

← Back to Portfolio

It hasn’t always been easy balancing my academic responsibilities and my passion for coding. There were times when schoolwork and personal projects competed for my attention.

What helped was building a schedule and setting clear goals. I dedicated time for both school and coding, making sure neither was neglected. It taught me discipline, time management, and how to stay motivated under pressure.

Looking back, the challenge made me stronger. I now see time as a resource that must be managed wisely to grow in both education and passion.
